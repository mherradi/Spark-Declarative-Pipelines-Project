# 🚖 Good Cabs Data Platform Modernization
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Good Cabs is a growing cab service company operating across multiple cities and regions. The company is committed to supporting local drivers while delivering excellent customer service.
As Good Cabs expands, regional operations teams increasingly rely on timely, region-specific data to manage daily activities such as driver allocation, ride monitoring, revenue tracking, and customer satisfaction analysis.
However, during a regional performance review, a critical issue surfaced:
Regional managers are not receiving data on time, and the dashboards provided are generic, slow, and difficult to use.
&nbsp;
&nbsp;

## 🚨 Business Problem

1️⃣ Delayed Data Delivery : Regional teams do not receive operational data on time, which cause Late reporting impacts

2️⃣ Generic, Non-Actionable Dashboards : The dashboards are Not region-specific, and Not optimized for operational decisions

3️⃣ Platform Architecture Limitations : The current data platform relies on Procedural Spark pipelines and Manual orchestration of workflows
