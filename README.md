# 🚖 Good Cabs Data Platform Modernization
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Good Cabs is a growing cab service company operating across multiple cities and regions. The company is committed to supporting local drivers while delivering excellent customer service.
As Good Cabs expands, regional operations teams increasingly rely on timely, region-specific data to manage daily activities such as driver allocation, ride monitoring, revenue tracking, and customer satisfaction analysis.
However, during a regional performance review, a critical issue surfaced:
Regional managers are not receiving data on time, and the dashboards provided are generic, slow, and difficult to use.
&nbsp;
&nbsp;

## 🚨 Business Problem

1️⃣ Delayed Data Delivery : Regional teams do not receive operational data on time, which cause Late reporting impacts

2️⃣ Generic, Non-Actionable Dashboards : The dashboards are Not region-specific, and Not optimized for operational decisions

3️⃣ Platform Architecture Limitations : The current data platform relies on Procedural Spark pipelines and Manual orchestration of workflows
&nbsp;
&nbsp;

## 📊 Expected Business Impact

🚀 Faster Regional Reporting : After modernization, data pipelines will process and deliver data much faster thanks to optimized execution and incremental updates.

🔄 Automated Incremental Updates : The new platform will process only new or changed data, rather than reprocessing entire datasets every time.

🧹 Reduced Manual Data Manipulation : Currently, regional teams export data and rework it manually (Excel, spreadsheets, etc.).

📍 Region-Specific Dashboards : Instead of generic dashboards, each region will have customized views tailored to their needs.


## 📌 Conclusion

GoodCabs’ challenges reflect the limits of a legacy data platform that cannot keep up with growing business demands. While stable, the system lacked the speed, flexibility, and scalability needed for effective regional operations.

By adopting a modern Lakehouse architecture on Databricks, this project transforms the platform into a more agile and scalable solution. With faster data delivery, automated processing, and region-specific insights, GoodCabs can better support decision-making and future growth.
